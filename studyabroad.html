Program Description

A program designed for ASU students of any major interested in participating in a powerful, individualized international internship in Dublin -- one of the most vibrant and friendly cities in Europe. In addition to working four days per week, students will enroll in a seminar-style course that focuses on Ireland's role in the global workplace.
