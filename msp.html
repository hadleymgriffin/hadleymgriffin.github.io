Hour of Code

Along with hosting workshops at ASU, once a year we get the opportunity to go to a local elementary school and teach code to grade school children!

This year, my co-worker and I went to Landmark Elementary school and taught Hour of Code to the 1st and 3rd graders. I loved their participation and enthusiasm as we had a conversation about how we use code to talk to computers, just like how we use words to talk to each other. At the end of the discussion, we told each of them that they would be coding their own Minecraft video game using code.org. Their eyes lit up!

I firmly believe that the earlier we introduce children to coding, the more spectacular our future will be!
